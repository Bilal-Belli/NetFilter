NetFilter Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

At NetFilter, we believe that your web browsing history is private. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information inside the NetFilter Chrome extension.

1. Local Processing Only

NetFilter performs all of its filtering operations directly on your computer. When you load a webpage:

  • The domain checking algorithm hashes and checks domains locally within the background service worker.
  • Your activity logs are stored on your local device using standard Google Chrome Storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).
  • We do not operate any external APIs or cloud databases to track your visits.

2. Data Collection

NetFilter does not collect, transmit, sell, or rent any of your browsing logs, visited URLs, block settings, or usage telemetry. Everything remains local on your device.

3. Storage Permissions

The extension uses the following Chrome Extension APIs:

  • storage: To save your active toggle settings, custom block lists, and activity history locally.
  • webNavigation: Intercepts connection attempts prior to page loading to determine if the target matches a blocked website. This inspection happens fully in memory.

4. User Rights & Data Deletion

You have full ownership of your data. You can delete your custom blocklist and clear all activity logs at any time from the NetFilter side panel dashboard. Uninstalling the extension automatically removes all associated storage blocks from your system.
