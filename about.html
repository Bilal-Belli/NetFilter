What is NetFilter?

NetFilter is a professional, high-performance browser extension developed by Bilal Belli. The primary purpose is to block access to distraction-heavy and harmful websites so you can keep focus and maintain a healthy digital life.

By preventing connections to bad sites before pages start resolving in your browser, NetFilter ensures a seamless and interruption-free experience.

Key Features

  • Pre-Navigation Filtering: Inspects navigation requests before they resolve to keep your browser fast and clean.
  • Default Protection: Includes blocks for 17,000+ domain hashes out-of-the-box.
  • Custom Blocklist: Add and delete custom domains to fit your unique requirements.
  • Activity Logs: Locally records intercepted site attempts so you can review statistics.
  • 100% Privacy Focused: No cloud synchronization or central APIs. All data belongs to you and remains on your hard drive.
